Nintendo Switch Serial Checker

An online tool to check your Nintendo Switch's compatibility with the RCM exploit.

Join Nintendo-Homebrew for further assistance. Join Le Homebrew France for helping us maintaining this guide.


			
Check your console's serial number under the console

			
Type your console's serial number below to check if it's compatible with the RCM exploit.

			

				
				
				
			

			
			
    
				
  • "Your console is not patched" means that your console is compatible with the Tegra RCM exploit, so it can be hacked without installing a chip.
    • 
				
  • "Your console is potentially patched" means that your console is supposedly compatible with the Tegra RCM exploit, but is not "hackable" without chip installation.
    • 
				
  • "Your console is patched" means that your console is incompatible with the Tegra RCM exploit, so it can't be hacked without installing a chip.
